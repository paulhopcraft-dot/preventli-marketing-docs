## Risk Management & Contingencies

### Technical Risks
**Risk:** Server overload on launch day  
**Mitigation:** Load testing, auto-scaling, backup servers ready
**Contingency:** Temporary user caps with waitlist

**Risk:** Critical bug discovered post-launch
**Mitigation:** Comprehensive testing, gradual rollout
**Contingency:** Rapid hotfix deployment, user communication

### Market Risks  
**Risk:** Competitor announces similar product during launch
**Mitigation:** Differentiated positioning, community focus
**Contingency:** Accelerate unique feature development

**Risk:** Lower than expected conversion rates
**Mitigation:** A/B test everything, user feedback loops
**Contingency:** Onboarding optimization, pricing adjustments

### Marketing Risks
**Risk:** Paid advertising performance below expectations
**Mitigation:** Multiple channel strategy, organic foundation
**Contingency:** Shift budget to performing channels

**Risk:** Community engagement lower than expected  
**Mitigation:** Active moderation, valuable content
**Contingency:** Increase community events, gamification
