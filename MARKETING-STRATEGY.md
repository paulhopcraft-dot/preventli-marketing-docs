## Content Marketing Strategy

### Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Prevention Education (40% of content)
**Goal:** Educate market on prevention value

**Content Types:**
- **"The Cost of Being Reactive"** - ROI calculators and case studies
- **Prevention Frameworks** - Methodologies and best practices
- **Industry Reports** - "State of Prevention in [Industry]" annual reports
- **Expert Interviews** - Prevention leaders and practitioners

#### Pillar 2: Product & Solution Content (30% of content)
**Goal:** Demonstrate Preventli capabilities

**Content Types:**
- **Feature Deep-Dives** - How-to guides and tutorials
- **Customer Success Stories** - Real prevention wins
- **Integration Guides** - Setup and optimization content
- **Product Updates** - Changelog and roadmap content

#### Pillar 3: Community & Culture (20% of content)
**Goal:** Build prevention community

**Content Types:**
- **Community Spotlights** - User-generated content features
- **Prevention Challenges** - Gamified community activities
- **Behind the Scenes** - Company culture and team content
- **User Stories** - Community member journeys

#### Pillar 4: Industry Insights (10% of content)
**Goal:** Thought leadership positioning

**Content Types:**
- **Trend Analysis** - Future of prevention technology
- **Regulatory Updates** - Compliance and prevention intersection
- **Competitive Analysis** - Market landscape insights
- **Executive Thought Leadership** - CEO/founder content

### Content Distribution

#### Primary Channels
1. **Company Blog** - 2-3 posts per week, SEO-optimized
2. **LinkedIn** - Daily posts, weekly long-form articles
3. **Discord Community** - Daily engagement, weekly AMAs
4. **Email Newsletter** - Weekly "Prevention Insights" 

#### Secondary Channels  
5. **Twitter/X** - Daily tips and insights
6. **YouTube** - Weekly educational videos
7. **Industry Publications** - Monthly guest articles
8. **Podcast Appearances** - 2-3 monthly guest spots

## Digital Marketing Strategy

### Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

**Target Keywords:**
- Primary: "prevention software", "proactive monitoring", "preventive analytics"
- Long-tail: "how to prevent [industry] problems", "prevention vs reactive costs"
- Local: "[city] prevention consulting", "prevention services near me"

**Content Strategy:**
- 50+ high-quality prevention guides and resources
- Industry-specific landing pages for each target segment
- Interactive tools: ROI calculators, prevention assessments

### Paid Advertising

#### Google Ads Strategy
**Budget:** $10K/month initially, scale to $50K/month
**Campaign Types:**
- Search campaigns for high-intent keywords
- Display campaigns for retargeting website visitors
- YouTube ads for educational content promotion

#### LinkedIn Ads Strategy
**Budget:** $15K/month initially, scale to $75K/month
**Campaign Types:**
- Sponsored content for thought leadership articles
- Lead gen forms for whitepaper downloads
- Event promotion for webinars and demos

#### Facebook/Meta Ads Strategy
**Budget:** $5K/month for community building
**Campaign Types:**
- Community growth ads for Discord server
- Video ads for educational content
- Lookalike audiences based on existing customers

### Email Marketing

#### Nurture Sequences
1. **New Subscriber Welcome** (7-email series over 2 weeks)
2. **Free Trial Onboarding** (5-email series over 1 week)  
3. **Prevention Education** (Monthly educational series)
4. **Win-Back Campaign** (3-email series for inactive users)

#### Newsletter Strategy
- **Weekly "Prevention Insights"** - Industry news, tips, case studies
- **Monthly Product Updates** - New features and improvements
- **Quarterly Industry Reports** - Deep-dive analysis and trends

## Measurement & Analytics

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

#### Brand Awareness
- **Brand mention volume** (social listening)
- **Organic search volume** for brand terms
- **Website direct traffic** growth
- **Earned media coverage** quantity and quality

#### Lead Generation  
- **Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs)** per month
- **Lead conversion rate** by channel
- **Cost per lead (CPL)** by campaign
- **Lead-to-customer conversion rate**

#### Community Growth
- **Discord server member growth**
- **Email subscriber growth rate**
- **Social media follower growth**  
- **User-generated content volume**

#### Revenue Attribution
- **Marketing-attributed revenue** by channel
- **Customer acquisition cost (CAC)** by source
- **Return on ad spend (ROAS)** by campaign
- **Customer lifetime value (CLV)** by acquisition source
