- Member relationship building
- Weekly community reports

**Senior Moderators (2-3)**
- Daily moderation and rule enforcement
- Conflict resolution and member support
- Content quality control

**Industry Experts (5-7)**
- Subject matter expertise in key verticals

- Lead industry-specific discussions
- Guest expert coordination
